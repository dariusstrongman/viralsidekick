FAQ

Do I need to give you access to my account?
No. The free scan runs entirely on public YouTube data — the same numbers anyone can see, analyzed properly. The deep retention autopsy (where people stop watching) needs a one-click YouTube connect, coming for Pro users.
Which platforms?
YouTube today. TikTok is next on the roadmap — Pro users get it first.

Your real uploads, your real numbers. The AI gets your actual titles, view counts, upload cadence and outliers, and reasons about your niche specifically. If a fix could apply to any channel, it doesn't make the report.
