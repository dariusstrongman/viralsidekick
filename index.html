what it does

Everything is grounded in your own channel

ViralSidekick is a web app for YouTube creators. You connect your channel, and it reads your real performance to give you a grade, explain why your videos over or under perform, show what is breaking out in your niche right now, and write your next move. No generic advice, every read is built from your uploads and your analytics.

A real grade

A single S to F score for how primed your channel is to break out, built from your outlier rate, cadence, consistency, and momentum.

Per-video coaching

We read each upload against your own median and tell you which ones worked, which flopped, and the exact fix for the next one.

Live niche radar

We sweep channels in your niche and surface the videos erupting right now, with the transferable mechanic you can use on your next one.

Live analytics

Connect your channel and we read your retention, click-through rate, and impressions so the coaching is based on real audience behavior.

why we ask for access to your youtube account

Read-only, yours alone, only to build your dashboard

When you connect your channel, ViralSidekick asks Google for two read-only permissions. We never post, edit, or delete anything on your channel. Here is exactly what each one is for.

https://www.googleapis.com/auth/youtube.readonly

Confirm you own the channel, and read it to build your dashboard

We call the YouTube Data API to verify that the Google account signing in actually owns the channel you are connecting, so nobody can bind a channel they do not control. We then read that channel and its uploaded videos to generate your grade and per-video coaching.

https://www.googleapis.com/auth/yt-analytics.readonly

Read your analytics so the coaching reflects real performance

We read your own YouTube Analytics: views, watch time, average view duration, audience retention, impressions, click-through rate, traffic sources, and demographics. This is the core of the product, we point out where retention drops off and which thumbnails have a low click-through rate, using your real numbers.

Read-only, we never change your channel Shown only to you, in your private dashboard Never sold or shared Revoke anytime, in-app or in your Google account

Our use of information received from Google APIs follows the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements, and the YouTube API Services Terms of Service. Full detail is in our privacy policy.
