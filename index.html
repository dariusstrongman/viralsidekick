Read-only, yours alone, only to build your dashboard

When you connect your channel, ViralSidekick asks Google for a single read-only permission. We never post, edit, or delete anything.

https://www.googleapis.com/auth/youtube.readonly

why we ask for access to your youtube account

Read-only, yours alone, only to build your dashboard

When you connect your channel, ViralSidekick asks Google for two read-only permissions. We never post, edit, or delete anything on your channel. Here is exactly what each one is for.

https://www.googleapis.com/auth/youtube.readonly

Confirm you own the channel, and read it to build your dashboard

We call the YouTube Data API to verify that the Google account signing in actually owns the channel you are connecting, so nobody can bind a channel they do not control. We then read that channel and its uploaded videos to generate your grade and per-video coaching.

https://www.googleapis.com/auth/yt-analytics.readonly

Read your analytics so the coaching reflects real performance

We read your own YouTube Analytics: views, watch time, average view duration, audience retention, impressions, click-through rate, traffic sources, and demographics. This is the core of the product, we point out where retention drops off and which thumbnails have a low click-through rate, using your real numbers.

questions

The honest answers

Is the free scan actually free?
Yes. Paste any channel and get a full grade and breakdown from public data, no signup and no card. Connecting your own channel for live analytics and unlimited coaching is the Pro tier, which starts with a 7-day free trial.
Do you post or change anything on my channel?
Never. We ask Google for a single read-only permission, purely to verify you own the channel and read your numbers. We cannot post, edit, or delete, and you can revoke access anytime from the app or your Google account.
What do I get that YouTube Studio does not give me?
Studio hands you raw charts. ViralSidekick reads every upload against your own median, turns it into one grade, tells you the exact second retention drops, sweeps your niche for what is breaking out right now, and ends every read with the specific move for your next video.
Does it work for small channels?
Yes. Everything is measured relative to your own median, not your subscriber count, so a 500-subscriber channel gets the same real coaching as a million-subscriber one.
Do you support TikTok too?
Yes. Connect TikTok alongside or instead of YouTube to read your reach, share rate, and posting performance in the same dashboard.
What does Pro cost?
Pro is $19 a month or $190 a year with a 7-day free trial. Team is $39.99 a month for one shared space with 5 seats. Grading channels stays free forever.
